📋 Game Rules

🎮 Objective:

Control the snake to eat fruits (yellow dots) and grow, avoiding collisions with walls or yourself.

⌨️ Controls:

Use the arrow keys (↑ ↓ ← →) or WASD keys to move the snake.

🍎 Eating Fruits:

Each fruit eaten adds 1 point and makes the snake grow.

❓ Questions:

Every 3 fruits consumed, you must answer a multiple-choice question to continue.

⚠️ Game Over:

The game ends if the snake collides with walls or itself.

✅ Feedback:

You will receive immediate feedback if your answer is correct or incorrect.
